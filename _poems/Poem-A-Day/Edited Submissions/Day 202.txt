	Day 202 by *a-random-quigle



I snagged my laces
on my feet,
they unlaced themselves-
I cursed!
And I ran, my laces
jangling-
clattering around.
Will they move like fingers,
curl about my legs?
Hung and stung,
will I be just like a spider
who has caught and numbed herself?
Should I keep myself for dinner?
Should I eat right away?
Oh the trouble&#039;s that you face
when you&#039;re hunting for yourself-
