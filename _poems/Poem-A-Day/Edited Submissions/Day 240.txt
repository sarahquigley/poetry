	Day 240 by *a-random-quigle



The folding people fit into those awkward places.
Do you ever wondered what that funny pocket
is for, the one you can never figure out
quite what to do with?

They crease so neatly-

dent proof, dust resistant,
conveniently portable,
easy wipe curves, shadow proof crannies,
death proof, sex proof...
buy quickly, now!
