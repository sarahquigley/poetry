	Day 279 by *a-random-quigle



From far across the water an eagle flew
dipping once in a while to pluck fish;
I planned to be dramatic and shout
she plucked my heart and sucked it down
her gullet-

that bird was sly and beautiful;
she stared me down through squinted eyes
and strolled,
every step letting me know
she was nowhere near hungry
as I-
